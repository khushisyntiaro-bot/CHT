STAAD Pro Course Content
Note: The syllabus may be updated to match current industry requirements and software advancements.

Introduction
Introduction to Structural Engineering and STAAD.Pro V8i fundamentals.

STAAD Basics
Getting familiar with the STAAD window and model generation using the STAAD editor.

3
Modeling Techniques
Snap node and beam concepts, Node/Beam editor workflows, and translational repeat model generation.

Properties & Loads
Assigning properties, supports, and loading conditions.

Structural Analysis
Structure analysis, simply supported beam analysis, and framed structure beam analysis.

Load Applications
Floor loads, wind analysis, seismic analysis, and load combinations.

Dynamic analysis, response spectrum analysis, and framed structure analysis including slabs.

8

Column and beam design, RCC design, staircase analysis, steel design, and rectangular water tank design.

9
Special Topics
Moving loads and foundation design.

10
Output & Reporting
Report generation and professional engineering output documentation.
